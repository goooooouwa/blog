--- layout: post title: 'Model, View, Controller analog in Backbone.js' categories: backbone.js --- Model, View, Controller analog in Backbone.js
Model: 
model( can bind model change to actions, including view re-rendering)
collection( model list)

View Controller: 
view( responsible for an area on the page)
event( view actions)

View: 
template( any, can be Underscore.js template)

View Transition: 
route( track state changes, view transitions)
