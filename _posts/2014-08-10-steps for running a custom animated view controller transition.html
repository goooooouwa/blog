--- layout: post title: 'steps for running a custom animated view controller transition' categories: Animation iOS User Interface --- steps for running a custom animated view controller transition
  1. set transitioning delegate( UIViewControllerTransitioningDelegate) who is responsible for managing the custom transition
  2. call presentViewController on presentingController to start the presenting process of the presented controller
  3. then the system will call animationControllerForPresentedController:presentingController:sourceController on your transitioning delegate to get the animator objects
  4. if return is not nil( meaning you have implemented a animationController)
  5. then the system will call transitionDuration on your animationController by passing in the view transition context( UIViewControllerContextTransitioning) to get the transition duration
  6. and then call animateTransition on your animationController by passing in the view transition context to actually animate the transition
  7. in your animateTransition method, you will setup the from and to view controller( not clear what this is), then you call [UIView animateWithDuration] (the actual UIViewAnimation APIs to render the animation) with a completeTransition block.
  8. finally when your custom transition animation is complete, the system will call your completeTransition on the context by passing in the animationController.
