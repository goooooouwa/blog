      I'm not a sick man living on electronic devices .  I'm beyond that .
      Computer is just a tool , the content on it is what we're seeking for .
      Programming is a pattern , I've sought for it , and I'm done. The pattern of programming is formal logic . You have to design the logic of your program , to make it functional . After that , you may have to make it efficient . And here comes the pattern of algorithm . And these two are the essential of programming .
      Mathematics is the subject that seeks for patterns . I love seeking patterns , that's what I do . So I'm a born mathematician . And the fact is I still need a lot of training to get me going . That's the price I have to pay , and it worths .
      So , next stage : seeking patterns .
