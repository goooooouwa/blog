非发布迭代 

- Velocity 22 
- 40% new features ~ 8 
- 10% BAU (existing feature enhancements) ~ 2 
- 20% tech ~ 4 
- 20% bug fixes ~ 4 
- 10% 其他（如on boarding API) ~ 2 

发布迭代: 

- 12 个点用于发布（env preparation, bug bash, bug fixes)
