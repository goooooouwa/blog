--- layout: post title: 'Democracy is not a silver bullet' --- Democracy is not a silver bullet
Democracy works when there's enough of people.
In essential, democracy is, the idea with most people support wins. In other word, the strongest opinion wins.
If the crowd is small, the strongest opinion is with no guarantee to be the best idea. This is why sometimes people say, the truth is from the minors.
The larger the crowd is, the more truth is in the strongest opinion.

So democracy is not always the best way to get the best idea. Especially in small crowd.
